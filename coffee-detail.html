Types of Coffee

Coffee is more than just a beverage; it's a lifestyle and a passion for many. Here is a list of different types of coffee that are enjoyed around the world.

  • Espresso - A strong, concentrated coffee served in small, strong shots.
  • Americano - A shot of espresso diluted with hot water, offering a more mellow flavor.
  • Latte - Made with a shot of espresso and steamed milk, topped with a small amount of froth.
  • Cappuccino - Similar to a latte but with a thicker layer of foam and sometimes topped with cocoa powder or cinnamon.
  • Mocha - A blend of coffee, steamed milk, and chocolate, often topped with whipped cream.
  • Macchiato - A shot of espresso with just a dash of foamed milk on top.
  • Flat White - Originating from Australia, it's similar to a latte but uses micro-foamed milk for a creamier texture.
  • Affogato - A dessert-like coffee where a scoop of vanilla ice cream is "drowned" in a shot of hot espresso.
